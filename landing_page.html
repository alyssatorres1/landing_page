Latino Mentor Program Mobile App

This app is designed to make members get rewarded, acknowledged, and appreciated with their volunteer efforts.

The Latino Mentor Program App is designed to help students in many mays to help them achieve success. The app inculdes many helpful tools to easily help out their community. The app inculdes a way to help students keep track of volunteer hours and even has a place where volunteer events or just regular events are posted. The app also inculdes news, a chat feature, school map, a place to schedule an advising appoitment, and most importantly a rewards feature. To explain the rewards feature, when a student hits a certain amount of volunteer hours, the student will recieve a reward to congraulate them on their help for the community. Rewards can range from gift cards, perks around campus etc.
